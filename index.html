Simple Skincare Routine

A basic 3-step routine to keep your skin healthy and glowing. Perfect for beginners or those who prefer minimal skincare.

1

Cleanse

Start with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and impurities without stripping your skin's natural moisture.

Recommended: CeraVe Hydrating Facial Cleanser or La Roche-Posay Toleriane Cleanser
2

Treat

Apply treatment products like serums or moisturizers with active ingredients tailored to your skin concerns.

Recommended: The Ordinary Niacinamide Serum (for oiliness) or CeraVe Moisturizing Cream (for dryness)
3

Protect

Always finish with SPF during the day to protect your skin from harmful UV rays and prevent premature aging.

Recommended: EltaMD UV Clear SPF 46 or Supergoop! Unseen Sunscreen
